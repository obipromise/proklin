FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have questions? Reach out via WhatsApp.

How quickly can you come?
We aim to offer same-day bookings in most Lagos areas, but arrival time depends on current availability and location. We'll provide an estimated window when you contact us – please confirm before making any irreversible plans.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
Our team typically brings their own equipment, but we recommend confirming at the time of booking. In rare cases (e.g., specific products you prefer), you may choose to provide supplies – we'll agree in writing beforehand.
Are your cleaners background-checked?
We conduct identity verification and basic reference checks as part of our hiring process. However, we do not claim any specific level of criminal background screening. Clients are advised to secure valuables and supervise as they see fit.
Is Proklin fully insured?
We maintain liability insurance within defined limits and terms. Coverage applies only to accidental damage directly caused by our team during active cleaning, and is subject to a claim process, deductible, and exclusions (e.g., pre-existing damage, wear and tear). Full policy details are available on request.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We take feedback seriously. If you're unhappy, contact us within 24 hours with clear photos/description. We will review and, at our discretion, offer a re-clean or partial adjustment. We do not offer refunds after 48 hours or if the issue results from unrealistic expectations.
How do I pay?
We accept bank transfers, cash, Opay, Palmpay, and Kuda. Payment is due immediately upon service completion – we do not offer credit. First-time clients may be asked for a small deposit depending on the job size.

Still have questions?

Our team is available during business hours (8am–8pm). We'll respond as soon as possible.
